Tutoring

Student-run programs and professional services



Student2Student

Student2Student is a free online tutoring service created by MVHS students for MVHS students that seeks to help peers and support teachers. Even more so, it is a collaborative modality that helps us stay connected, engaged, and feel supported by our MVHS community.

Inara Middle School Mentoring

Inara is an organization dedicated to keeping middle school students engaged and motivated during quarantine as well as providing guidance through the transition from middle to high school. Currently recruiting motivated high school volunteers to serve as mentors for middle school students in the area. As Student Mentors, selected volunteers will have the opportunity to share their experience with younger students, earn volunteer hours, and develop interpersonal skills.

MV Helps

A program run by Mountain View residents for helping other at risk residents or healthcare professionals. Volunteering includes grocery runs, deliveries, and other related services. Seeking low risk, healthy individuals without immunocompromised family.

Red Cross Blood Donation

In coordination with the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), the Red Cross is seeking people who are fully recovered from the new coronavirus to sign up to donate plasma to help current COVID-19 patients. Patients who have fully recovered following a COVID-19 diagnosis may have antibodies in their plasma that can help those with serious or immediately life-threatening COVID-19 infections.

Engaging with the Elders

Looking for talented volunteers willing to record some aspect of their talent (singing, poetry, dancing, etc.) to share online with the elderly. As a result of the Covid 19 pandemic many elders in nursing homes and care facilities are feeling increasingly isolated. The aim is to help combat the loneliness of our elders.
